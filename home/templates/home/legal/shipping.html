UniversalWebinars provides digital delivery and hassle-free returns for your learning journey. All products are delivered instantly through your account — no waiting, no shipping fees.

1. Shipping Policy (Digital Delivery)

UniversalWebinars specializes in digital education products including online courses, webinars, downloadable resources, and interactive training modules.

Instant Access

  • Immediate access after payment confirmation
  • Access via your account dashboard
  • Direct download links where applicable

Global Availability

  • Available worldwide 24/7
  • No geographical restrictions
  • Multi-language support where available

For access issues, contact support@universalwebinars.com with your order details for resolution within 24 hours.

Digital delivery only • No shipping fees • No waiting periods

2. Return & Refund Policy

We stand behind the quality of our digital education products with a satisfaction guarantee.

Refund Window

  • Full refund within 5 working days of purchase
  • No questions asked within the first 5 days
  • Processed within 5–10 business days

Refund Eligibility

  • Content not downloaded or accessed beyond preview
  • Request submitted within the eligible period
  • Valid payment method used for original purchase

3. Cancellations

Webinar Cancellations

  • 48+ hours notice — full refund issued
  • 24–48 hours notice — 50% refund
  • Within 24 hours — non-refundable

Subscription Cancellations

  • Cancellation effective at end of current billing cycle
  • No refunds for unused partial periods
  • Downloadable resources may be non-refundable once accessed

Requests can be submitted via email or your account dashboard. Emergency situations reviewed case-by-case.

4. Technical Issues

Our support team is available to help with any access problems you experience.

  • Initial response within 2 hours during business hours
  • Most issues resolved within 24 hours
  • Contact support@universalwebinars.com with your order number and device details

Common Solutions

  • Clear browser cache and cookies
  • Try another browser or device
  • Check your internet connection
  • Disable ad-blockers temporarily

5. How to Request a Return

  1. Email support@universalwebinars.com with subject "Return Request"
  2. Provide your order number, product name, and reason
  3. Our team will review and process within 5–10 business days

Required Information

  • Full name and account email address
  • Order number or transaction ID
  • Date of purchase
  • Reason for the return request
  • Screenshots if applicable

6. Exceptions

  • Content downloaded, streamed, or accessed beyond preview
  • Requests submitted after the eligible window
  • Policy violations or fraudulent activity

Exceptions for technical access issues are reviewed individually with documentation.

7. Policy Amendments

  • Email notification 30 days before changes take effect
  • Website banner announcements for major updates
  • Updated date displayed on this page

Continued use after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
